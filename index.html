Munnar

Munnar is a picturesque hill station in the states of Kerala. The erstwhile summer resort of the British government, it is nestled at the point of intersection of the Kundala, Mudrapuzha and Nallathanni Rivers. The hills, covered by a blanket of tea plantations under the blue clouds that play hide and seek with the sun, make it a haven for admirers of nature.
